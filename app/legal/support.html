Common questions

How does the watering schedule work?
Plantaroo starts from a sensible interval for each plant (adjusted for your soil mix and light) and then learns from your real watering history, nudging the schedule toward how you actually care for the plant.

Why isn't my reminder showing?
Make sure notifications are enabled in Settings → Watering reminders, and that you've allowed notifications for Plantaroo in your phone's Settings.

How do I fix a mistaken watering?
Long-press the event in the History tab (or in a plant's Recent activity) to remove it — the schedule recalculates automatically.

How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all your plants, photos, and history.
