Plantaroo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Plantaroo is a plant-care tracker. We built it to be quiet and respectful of your data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

What we collect

  • Account info — when you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your email address (or Apple's private relay email) and a unique user ID. We use these only to create your account and sync your data.
  • Your plant data — the plants you add and their details (name, room, care settings, notes, watering/feeding history) and any photos you choose to attach.

We do not collect your location, contacts, advertising identifiers, or analytics about how you use the app. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

How your data is stored

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) under your account and is readable only by you. It is also cached on your device so the app works offline. We never sell your data and never share it with third parties for advertising.

Notifications

Watering reminders are scheduled entirely on your device. They are not sent from a server and contain no data about you.

Your choices

  • Delete everything — Settings → Delete account permanently removes your account and all associated plants, photos and history.
  • Turn off reminders — Settings → Watering reminders.
  • Sign out at any time; your data stays safely in your account until you return.

Children

Plantaroo is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above. Continued use after a change means you accept the updated policy.
